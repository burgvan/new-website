About me

My name is Vanessa. For 7+ years, I have been designing solid, holistic user experiences and have done so for web, mobile, TV, and Internet of Things related platforms. With a strong UX/UI design background in conjunction with some evaluative research skills, I have a natural understanding of ideal experiences that meets users' needs without making them think too much about how to reach their goals and meet their needs when interacting with a digital product or service. Problem-solving and designing are both what I'm hugely passionate about the most in my career. I also believe designing solid cross-channel experiences with branding kept in mind is key to a successful business.

Aside from being a bit of a nerd, I'm also a music lover, an animal lover, and a nature-loving hiker.
